1. Find all the angles between $0^{\circ}$ and $360^{\circ}$ such that
(a) $\sec x=2.5$
(b) $\quad \operatorname{cosec} x=-1.5$
2. Solve the equation $\cot y=3$ for $-\pi<y<\pi$.
3. Solve the equation $\sec ^{2} x+\tan x=1$ for $0 \leq x \leq 2 \pi$.
4. (a) Solve the equation $\cot x=\sin x$ for $0^{\circ} \leq x \leq 360^{\circ}$.
(b) Sketch the graphs of $y=\cot x$ and $y=\sin x$ for $0^{\circ} \leq x \leq 360^{\circ}$ on the same axes, and indicate the roots found in part (i) on your graph.
5. A function is defined by $\mathrm{f}(x)=2 \arccos x-1$.
(a) Write down the maximum domain and range of this function.
(b) Find the exact value of $\mathrm{f}(-0.5)$.
(c) Find the inverse function $\mathrm{f}^{-1}(x)$.
6. (a) Prove that $(\sec \theta-\tan \theta)^{2} \equiv \frac{1-\sin \theta}{1+\sin \theta}$.
(b) Hence solve the equation
$$
2(\sec \theta-\tan \theta)^{2}=-3 \operatorname{cosec} \theta
$$
for $0 \leq \theta \leq 2 \pi$.
